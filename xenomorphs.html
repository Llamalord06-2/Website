The Xenomorphs

What Makes them so dangerous

What makes the xenomorphs so dangerous is their adaptability. they have the ability to take traits from previous hosts, this could be memories, routines, or even recognising their host's siblings or relatives.

Another factor that makes them dangerous is the fact that there are a lot of variations of the species, such as

  • Xenomorph Queen
  • Drone
  • Warrior
  • Runner
  • Chest Burster
  • Face Hugger
  • Egg

Most powerful hosts

  1. Predalien:
    A cross between a Yaujta and a Xenomorph. it has the positives of both species, good hunting skills, Technology, Acid blood and Adaptability. There has been many Predaliens over the franchise. They are seen as a dishonor to both their species and always hunted by both them.

  2. Mr Sinister Xenomorph:
    During a crossover with Marvel, there was an issue, where the Mutant, Mr Sinister, is taken over by a xenomorph, which gained powers. These powers include, Teleportation from Nightcrawler, Enhanced Durability from Colosus and supposedly telepathy. making it overall dangerous

  3. TMNT Xenomorph(Fan Made):
    There are various fan made versions of the xenomorph, one of these is the Turtle-Morph, a fusion of a Ninja turtle and a Xeno. This makes for a scary alien, because it retained its martial arts skills and weapon ability. making the xenomorph even more stealthy and deadly.

    The ultramorph is one of the most deadly xenos in the franchise, due to it being over 16+ ft tall. fast, and the most intelligent of the species. Due to the fact that he was spawned from an engineer, the smartest species in the universe, this enhances its deadliness because it can outthink
