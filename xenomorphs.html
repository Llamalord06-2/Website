The Xenomorphs

Introduction/Origin

The Xenomorphs are rumoured to be synthetic/genetically modified bio weapons, created by the engineers, or some other faction. They were supposed to be a bio weapon used to kill and infect othere species, but evolved further and became their own creatures

What Makes them so dangerous

What makes the xenomorphs so dangerous is their adaptability. they have the ability to take traits from previous hosts, this could be memories, routines, or even recognising their host's siblings or relatives.

Another factor that makes them dangerous is the fact that there are a lot of variations of the species, such as

  • Xenomorph Queen
  • Drone
  • Warrior
  • Runner
  • Chest Burster
  • Face Hugger
  • Egg
